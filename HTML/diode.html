DIODE
1.A diode is a two-terminal electronic component that conducts current primarily in one direction
2.It has low (ideally zero) resistance in one direction, and high (ideally infinite) resistance in the other.
3.A diode thermionic diode is a vacuum tube with two electrodes.
4.A semiconductor diode, the most commonly used type today.
5.Semiconductor diodes were the first semiconductor electronic devices.
6.Diodes are all considered passive devices.
